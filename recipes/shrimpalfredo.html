Shrimp Alfredo

Description:

A delicious and easy quick-to-come-together weeknight dinner! Plump, juicy shrimp perfectly cooked and seasoned, smothered in delicious cheesy alfredo sauce, and a healthy serving of veggies mixed in to balance the creamy indulgence!

Ingredients:

  • Jar of your favorite alfredo (I love Bertolli's Garlic Alfredo!)
  • 12 oz thawed or fresh shrimp
  • Favorite pasta shape
  • Spinach, chopped
  • Tomato, diced
  • Shredded parmesan (to top)

Directions:

  1. Start by boiling a pot of salted water for pasta. While that gets going, season shrimp and heat a pan.
  2. When pan is heated, cook shrimp 2-3 minutes on each side, until firm and opaque pink.
  3. Remove shrimp, your water should be boiling by now. Cook the pasta to al dente.
  4. Add diced tomatoes to same pan as shrimp and cook down for 1-2 minutes. Add sauce.
  5. When sauce starts to bubble, add spinach and stir until cooked down.
  6. Drain pasta. Combine pasta, shrimp, and sauce. Enjoy!
